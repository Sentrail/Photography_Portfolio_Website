About Me

Hello! I'm Alex, a passionate photographer based in New York. For over 8 years, I've been freezing moments in time, telling stories through my lens.

My journey began with an old film camera, and the magic of capturing light has never left me. I believe that every photo should evoke an emotion, whether it's the joy of a wedding day, the serenity of a mountain peak, or the unique personality in a portrait.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me hiking, exploring new cafes, or planning my next photographic adventure. Let's create something beautiful together.
